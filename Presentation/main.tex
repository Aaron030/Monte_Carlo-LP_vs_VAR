    \begin{itemize}
        \item<1-> VARs and Local Projection used for Impulse Response Analysis, think Unemployment and Interest Rate, Oil Prices and Inflation, ...
        \item<2-> finite sample comparison (VAR(p) vs. LP(p))
        \begin{enumerate}
            \item<2-> In finite samples, bias-variance puzzle 
            \item<2-> VARs more efficient, LPs more robust to misspecification 
        \end{enumerate}
        \item<3-> In Population both estimate the same Impulse Response
        \item<4-> Ludwig identity shows that equal lag comparison is inherently flawed 
    \end{itemize}
